About

Dr. Llamas received her Bachelor’s of Science in Biology and Microbiology from California State University, Los Angeles. She then conducted research for pharmaceutical clinical trials at the University of Southern California, before earning her Doctorate of Naturopathic Medicine from Bastyr University.

She has completed clinical rotations at Seattle Children’s Hospital, Children’s Hospital of Los Angeles, and various community outreach clinics in Southern California and Washington. She is fluent in both Spanish and English.
